Tommy Flowers

(Dec 22, 1905 - Oct 28, 1998)

Lots of people never heard about Tommy Flowers but he is one of the greatest heroes of World War Two. Although he never joined the battlefield, Tommy Flowers used his invention to save many thousands of lives. Tommy Flowers is one of histories most significant electrical engineers. His work on the first programmable computer, Colossus, has sealed his place in history.
